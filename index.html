Oklahoma Football

A Fan Page

The Oklahoma Sooners football team is one of the most successful college football programs in the country. I have been a fan for as long as I remember. Sitting in front of our old RCA console TV in the living room on Thanksgiving day watching OU and Nebraska battle for the Big 8 and National championships are some of my fondest memories. I would marvel at the ability of great players like Billy Simms, Joe Washington, and Steve Davis. I love watching the OU Sooners play football!

    Football Accomplishments

  • All-time Record: 899-324-53(.725)
  • National Championships: 7
  • Conference Titles: 47
  • Heisman Trophy Winners: 6
  • Consensus All-Americans: 80
  • College Football HOF inductees: 23

Wins

The program began in 1895(That's 12 years before Oklahoma even became a state!) and is one of the most successful programs since World War II with the most wins (606) and the highest winning percentage (.762) since 1945

Champions

The program has 7 national championships, 45 conference championships, 162 First Team All-Americans (80 consensus), and six Heisman Trophy winners.

Quality

The school has had 23 members (five coaches and 18 players) inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame and holds the record for the longest winning streak in Division I history with 47 straight victories.
